Welcome!




My name is Christian and I am a Chicago-based portrait and events photographer. I am driven to capture moments that feel fresh and bold.


I started my journey back in high school with a film camera, and a few years later with my first DSLR. Through many years of practice, I have become passionate about using available light and contrast in creating energetic and emotional stories.


Outside of photography, I work full-time in analytics, rollerblade, work out, and dance to salsa. Anything that keeps me moving gets me further inspired in my creative flow.


Every time I go out and shoot, I challenge myself creatively by finding different angles, light, and moments that make your moments stand out!
